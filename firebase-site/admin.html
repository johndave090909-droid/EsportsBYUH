Go live on the club's Facebook page first. Then copy the live video's link (tap ⋯ on the live post → Copy link), paste it below, tick the box, and SAVE — the stream appears at the top of the home page. When the stream ends, untick the box and save again.

Matches Page

The Schedule and Results tabs, the Standings table, and the home page's "Upcoming Matches" are all built automatically from the Bracket — edit teams, scores, dates, and locations there. This page only controls the text at the top of the Matches page and the register banner.

Page Text & Register Banner

The register button's link is set in Site Settings → "Register link".

Bracket

This is the one place to run a tournament: the Schedule tab, Results tab, Standings table, and the home page's "Upcoming Matches" all fill in automatically from what you enter here. Each match card has the two teams and scores on top, and its date & location underneath. Round names are editable and become the "stage" shown on the site; a blank team slot shows as TBD, a slot opposite a team as BYE; fill both scores and the match counts as played (it moves from Schedule to Results and updates Standings). Nothing changes on the site until you press SAVE BRACKET.

News

Posts are listed newest first. One post can be pinned — it shows in the highlighted box at the top (with its optional link button). The three newest posts also appear on the home page.
